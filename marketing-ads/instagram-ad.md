## Instagram Ad Best Practices

**Visual Guidelines:**
- Use high-quality, bright images
- Maintain consistent color scheme
- Include text overlays for key info
- Use Instagram's native aspect ratios
- Square (1:1) for feed posts
- Vertical (9:16) for stories/reels
- Horizontal (16:9) for IGTV

**Content Strategy:**
- Post 2-3 times per week
- Mix property photos with floor plans
- Share behind-the-scenes content
- Use Instagram Stories daily
- Create Reels for engagement
- Use carousel posts for details

**Engagement Tips:**
- Respond to DMs within 2 hours
- Use Instagram Stories polls
- Share provider testimonials
- Post property updates
- Use location tags
- Tag relevant accounts

**Hashtag Strategy:**
- Use 10-15 relevant hashtags
- Mix popular and niche hashtags
- Include location-based hashtags
- Use AFH-specific hashtags
- Research competitor hashtags

**Instagram Shopping (if applicable):**
- Set up product catalog
- Tag products in posts
- Enable shopping on posts
- Use shopping stickers in stories
