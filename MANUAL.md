## Introduction

### What is AFH Property Advisor?

The AFH Property Advisor is a comprehensive web application designed to assist property owners in successfully leasing their properties to Adult Family Home (AFH) providers. The application provides tools for scenario analysis, property assessment, timeline planning, budget management, marketing, and provider search.

### Who Should Use This Application?

- Property owners looking to lease to AFH providers
- Real estate investors interested in AFH properties
- Property managers handling AFH conversions
- Anyone involved in AFH property development

### Key Benefits

- **Comprehensive Analysis:** Detailed scenario comparison with pros, cons, and cost analysis
- **Visual Planning:** Interactive floor plan with room-by-room analysis
- **Budget Management:** Detailed cost tracking with landlord/tenant sharing
- **Marketing Support:** Ready-to-use templates and scripts
- **Provider Resources:** Comprehensive search tools and screening guides

## Scenarios Module

### Overview

The Scenarios module presents three comprehensive approaches to leasing your property to AFH providers. Each scenario includes detailed analysis, timelines, budgets, and implementation steps.

### Scenario 1: Direct Provider Lease

**When to Use:**
- You need revenue quickly
- Limited capital for conversion
- Willing to accept lower rent initially
- Property is in good condition

**Detailed Analysis:**

**Pros:**
- **Fastest Path to Revenue:** Can find a provider in 30-60 days
- **No Upfront Costs:** Landlord invests $0-$5,000 (minor repairs only)
- **Provider Handles Conversion:** Tenant manages all permits and modifications
- **Lower Risk:** Less financial exposure for landlord
- **Flexible Terms:** Can negotiate based on provider's needs
- **Quick Start:** Provider can begin operations faster

**Cons:**
- **Limited Provider Pool:** Fewer providers willing to take on full conversion
- **Longer Lease Commitment:** May need to offer 7-10 year terms
- **Lower Rent Potential:** Typically $2,500-$3,500/month
- **Less Control:** Provider makes conversion decisions
- **Potential Quality Issues:** Less oversight of conversion work
- **Longer Provider Search:** May take 60-90 days to find right provider

**Implementation Steps:**

1. **Week 1-2: Initial Research**
   - Search AFH locator for providers in Centralia/Chehalis area
   - Identify providers with 4-6 beds (expansion candidates)
   - Research provider backgrounds and track records
   - Note providers who have been operating 3+ years

2. **Week 3-4: Community Engagement**
   - Join Facebook AFH communities (WA AFH Providers, etc.)
   - Introduce yourself and your property opportunity
   - Post property listing with floor plan
   - Engage with existing discussions

3. **Week 5-6: Active Outreach**
   - Contact identified providers directly
   - Send property information and floor plan
   - Schedule property viewings
   - Follow up on all inquiries

4. **Week 7-8: Provider Screening**
   - Screen interested providers using provided questions
   - Check references and licenses
   - Evaluate financial stability
   - Assess provider experience and track record

5. **Week 9-10: Negotiations**
   - Negotiate lease terms (5-10 year term recommended)
   - Discuss rent structure and adjustments
   - Clarify conversion responsibilities
   - Review maintenance and insurance requirements

6. **Week 11-12: Lease Execution**
   - Finalize lease agreement with attorney review
   - Execute lease documents
   - Coordinate property handover
   - Begin lease term

**Marketing Approach:**
- Target existing providers looking to expand or relocate
- Emphasize property's potential and location
- Highlight flexibility in lease terms
- Focus on providers with conversion experience

**Expected Timeline:** 30-90 days from start to lease execution

**Budget Breakdown:**
- Property listing fees: $0-$200
- Marketing materials: $100-$300
- Legal review: $500-$1,500
- Minor repairs: $0-$5,000
- **Total: $600-$7,000**

### Scenario 2: Minimal WABO Conversion (RECOMMENDED)

**When to Use:**
- You have moderate capital ($25K-$40K)
- Want to attract more qualified providers
- Seeking higher rent potential
- Prefer property to be "turnkey" ready

**Detailed Analysis:**

**Pros:**
- **Turnkey Property:** Property becomes ready for immediate operation
- **Higher Rent Potential:** $3,500-$5,000/month achievable
- **More Qualified Providers:** Attracts established, professional providers
- **Faster Provider Onboarding:** Provider can start operations immediately
- **Better Lease Terms:** Can negotiate more favorable terms
- **Property Value Increase:** Conversion adds permanent value
- **Shared Risk:** Cost-sharing reduces landlord exposure

**Cons:**
- **Upfront Investment:** Requires $25,000-$40,000 investment
- **Conversion Timeline:** 60-90 days for completion
- **Contractor Coordination:** Need to manage construction process
- **Permit Delays:** Potential for inspection delays
- **Cost Overruns:** Risk of exceeding budget
- **Market Timing:** Need to time conversion with provider search

**Implementation Steps:**

1. **Days 1-15: Planning & Permits**
   - Research WABO requirements thoroughly
   - Obtain complete permit checklist from local authorities
   - Hire architect/engineer for compliance drawings ($2,000)
   - Submit permit applications (WABO, building, fire) ($1,500)
   - Obtain contractor bids (3-5 contractors for comparison)

2. **Days 16-25: Contractor Selection**
   - Review all contractor bids in detail
   - Check contractor references and licenses
   - Verify insurance and bonding
   - Negotiate contract terms and cost-sharing
   - Finalize contract with selected contractor
   - Obtain insurance certificates

3. **Days 26-50: Bathroom Construction**
   - Demolition of existing bathroom fixtures ($1,500)
   - Install ADA-compliant toilets and fixtures ($12,000)
   - Install grab bars and safety features ($3,000)
   - Install roll-in shower capabilities
   - Install non-slip flooring ($3,000)
   - Bathroom inspections and corrections ($500)

4. **Days 51-70: Common Areas**
   - Install handrails in living, dining, den areas ($4,000)
   - Kitchen modifications (accessible counters, fire suppression) ($5,000)
   - Install emergency call systems in all rooms ($3,000)
   - Patio ramp installation and handrails ($4,000)
   - Lighting improvements throughout

5. **Days 71-85: Inspections & Certification**
   - WABO preliminary inspection ($500)
   - Address inspection corrections ($2,000)
   - Fire department inspection ($300)
   - Final WABO certification and permit issuance ($500)

6. **Days 86-120: Marketing & Provider Search**
   - Create professional marketing materials ($500)
   - Post on AFH locator websites and Facebook ($200)
   - Screen interested providers (35 days)
   - Negotiate lease terms with selected provider
   - Execute lease agreement ($1,000)

**Marketing Approach:**
- Market as "WABO-ready" property
- Emphasize turnkey nature and immediate occupancy
- Target both existing and new providers
- Highlight cost-sharing arrangement benefits

**Expected Timeline:** 90-120 days total (60-90 conversion + 30 provider search)

**Budget Breakdown:**
- Planning and permits: $3,500
- Bathroom construction: $20,000
- Common area modifications: $16,000
- Inspections: $1,300
- Marketing: $1,700
- **Total: $42,500**
- **Landlord Share (70%): $29,750**
- **Tenant Share (30%): $12,750**

### Scenario 3: Full Conversion + Long-term Lease

**When to Use:**
- You have significant capital ($50K-$75K)
- Seeking maximum return on investment
- Want premium, long-term tenants
- Property is long-term investment

**Detailed Analysis:**

**Pros:**
- **Maximum Rent Potential:** $5,000-$7,000/month achievable
- **Premium Providers:** Attracts top-tier providers with strong track records
- **Long-Term Stability:** 10+ year leases common
- **Property Value Appreciation:** Significant increase in property value
- **Low Maintenance:** Fully converted properties require less ongoing work
- **Competitive Advantage:** Stand out in market with premium offering
- **Higher Occupancy Rates:** Premium providers maintain better occupancy

**Cons:**
- **Highest Investment:** Requires $50,000-$75,000 upfront
- **Longest Timeline:** 120-180 days to completion
- **Complex Project Management:** Multiple contractors and phases
- **Higher Risk:** More capital at risk
- **Market Dependency:** Need strong AFH market to justify investment
- **Longer Payback Period:** Takes longer to recoup investment

**Implementation Steps:**

1. **Days 1-30: Planning & Design**
   - Hire architect for full AFH design ($5,000)
   - Complete comprehensive architectural drawings
   - Submit all permit applications ($2,500)
   - Permit review and approval process

2. **Days 31-80: Major Construction**
   - Structural modifications if needed ($15,000)
   - Electrical upgrades (emergency systems, outlets) ($8,000)
   - HVAC modifications for proper ventilation ($6,000)
   - Plumbing upgrades for all bathrooms ($10,000)
   - Fire suppression system installation ($8,000)

3. **Days 81-120: Room Finishes & ADA Compliance**
   - All bathroom conversions (ADA-compliant) ($20,000)
   - Resident room modifications (handrails, call systems) ($12,000)
   - Common area modifications (handrails, lighting) ($8,000)
   - Kitchen and dining area full conversion ($10,000)

4. **Days 121-150: Final Systems & Inspections**
   - Install all safety systems (call buttons, alarms) ($5,000)
   - WABO preliminary and follow-up inspections ($1,000)
   - Fire department and building inspections ($800)
   - Address all inspection corrections ($5,000)
   - Final certifications and permit issuance ($1,200)

5. **Days 151-210: Premium Provider Search**
   - Professional photography and marketing ($2,000)
   - List on premium AFH platforms ($500)
   - Screen and interview premium providers (30 days)
   - Negotiate long-term lease (10+ years) (15 days)
   - Finalize lease and provider onboarding ($2,000)

**Marketing Approach:**
- Target established providers with multiple locations
- Emphasize premium, turnkey nature
- Highlight long-term partnership opportunity
- Focus on providers with strong financials

**Expected Timeline:** 120-180 days total (90-120 conversion + 30-60 provider search)

**Budget Breakdown:**
- Planning and design: $7,500
- Major construction: $47,000
- Room finishes: $50,000
- Final systems: $13,000
- Marketing: $4,500
- **Total: $122,000**
- **Landlord Share (80%): $97,600**
- **Tenant Share (20%): $24,400**

### Cost-Sharing Rationale

**Landlord Pays 100%:**
- Permits and inspections (add permanent value to property)
- Initial design and planning (property-specific)
- Legal and consulting fees
- Marketing materials (property promotion)

**Landlord Pays 70-80%:**
- Construction and modifications (major improvements)
- Structural changes (permanent value)
- Safety systems (property requirement)
- Major fixtures and equipment

**Tenant Pays 20-30%:**
- Operational improvements
- Tenant-specific customizations
- Furniture and equipment (if not included)
- Operational systems setup

**Shared 50/50:**
- Contract negotiations (mutual benefit)
- Lease finalization (both parties)
- Property tours (mutual interest)
